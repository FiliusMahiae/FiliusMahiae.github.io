Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Random Fact Generator?

Random Fact Generator is a platform where you can discover random facts across different categories. Whether you're looking for fun trivia, historical facts, or scientific discoveries, you'll always learn something new with each click.

How often is the content updated?

I personally update the database regularly with new and fascinating facts to keep things fresh and engaging. I'm always on the lookout for interesting additions to the collection, so there's always something new to discover!

How does the random fact generation work?

Our platform uses a custom-built API to fetch random facts from a database. Every time you click "Give me a Fact!", the API retrieves a new fact and displays it instantly on the screen.
